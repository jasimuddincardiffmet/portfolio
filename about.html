EM-RFMIC Engineering Research Group

Delivering Wales leadership in the areas of RF, Microwave and Electromagnetics Engineering to drive innovation in Healthcare and Space Systems

EM-RFMic research group was established by Dr. Jasim Uddin in mid 2023 and operates within the Centre for Engineering Research Intelligent Sensors and Systems (CeRISS). The group is primarily focuses in three key areas of research: Space Engineering, healthcare, and wireless technologies. The overall goal is to actively contribute to Wales advancement by positively impacting the community, leveraging expertise, and strive to deliver cutting-edge solutions that drive progress and enhance the quality of life and create a brighter future for everyone.

Research Themes:

    Space Engineering
    Healthcare
    Wireless Technologies
