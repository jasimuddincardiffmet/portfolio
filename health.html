Healthcare Technologies

The EM-RFMic group is a dynamic and innovative team dedicated to advancing the field of healthcare technologies, with a focus on electromagnetics, RF, and microwave engineering, our research encompasses a wide range of applications in the realm of healthcare technologies including ECG, EMG, EEG, and blood pressure flex sensors. Our group firmly believes in the transformative wireless power of technology in healthcare, and we strive to develop cutting-edge solutions that can revolutionize in 24 hours patient care monitoring and diagnostics. By leveraging our expertise in electromagnetic theory and design, we are able to create novel devices and systems that address the unique challenges faced in healthcare settings. From wearable sensors and wireless communication systems to IoT capabilities and therapeutic devices, our research spans a broad spectrum of healthcare technologies. We collaborate closely with medical professionals, industry partners, and external academia to ensure that our solutions are not only technologically advanced but also clinically relevant and impactful.
