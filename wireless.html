6G, and Nonterrestrial Network (NTN)

EM-RFMic Research Group is at the forefront of pioneering research in wireless communication, 6G, nonterrestrial technologies, and reflective intelligent surface (Ris) With a specific focus on power splitters, LNA, Band pass/stop Filters, transmitters, receivers, transceivers, and attenuators, and reflective intelligent surface. Our group strives to advance the capabilities and efficiency of 5G wireless communication systems. Through rigorous theoretical analysis, innovative design methodologies, and extensive experimental investigations, we aim to develop state-of-the-art solutions that address key challenges in terrestrial wireless communication. Our research encompasses a broad range of applications, including but not limited to telecommunications, satellite communication, IoT (Internet of Things), and next-generation wireless networks.
